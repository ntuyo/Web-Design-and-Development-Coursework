Getting Started
To kick start your career as a software developer, taking some CS classes at CRLS is a smart choice.
CRLS has a very wide selection of classes availible, but some good choices are the following:
  • AP Computer Science Principles
  • AP Computer Science
Some out of school activities availible are:
  • Joining a local CS club
  • Applying for Internships
  • Summer CS programs
